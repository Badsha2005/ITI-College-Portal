About Our Internship Program

Barasat-1 Govt ITI partners with leading companies to provide valuable internship opportunities for our students. These internships offer hands-on experience, professional development, and potential career pathways in various technical fields.

Benefits

  • Practical industry experience
  • Skill development
  • Networking opportunities
  • Potential job offers

Eligibility

  • Current ITI students
  • Minimum 75% attendance
  • Good academic standing
  • Completed basic coursework

Duration

  • Typically 2-6 months
  • Full-time during breaks
  • Part-time during semester
  • Flexible schedules
