About Us

Barasat 1 Government ITI College, located in the North 24 Parganas district of West Bengal, is a government-run technical training institution affiliated with NCVT (National Council for Vocational Training).

The institute is dedicated to providing high-quality vocational education and skill development to empower the youth of the region. With a range of certified ITI courses, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and experienced faculty, Barasat 1 Govt. ITI prepares students for careers in modern industries and services.

Our mission is to enhance employability by offering hands-on training and developing a technically skilled workforce to support the industrial growth of the state and nation.
