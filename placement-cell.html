Our Placement Process

1

Registration & Profile Creation

Students register with the placement cell and create their professional profiles with academic and skill details.

2

Training & Preparation

Comprehensive training including technical skills, resume building, interview preparation, and soft skills.

3

Company Presentations

Recruiters present their company profiles, job roles, and expectations to interested students.

4

Written Tests

Companies conduct aptitude, technical, or psychometric tests to shortlist candidates.

5

Interviews

Technical and HR interviews conducted by company representatives.

6

Job Offers

Selected candidates receive offer letters with details of compensation and joining dates.
